About Us

Khalsa Bakers has been a symbol of purity and tradition since 1990. With generations of baking expertise, we offer the finest bakery items, crafted from high-quality ingredients in a state-of-the-art, hygienic environment.

Our founder, Sardar Harpreet Singh, started this bakery with a simple mission: to bring authentic, wholesome baked goods to our community. Today, we continue that tradition with the same dedication to quality and service.
